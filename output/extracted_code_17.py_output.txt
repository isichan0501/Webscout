 The capital of France is Paris. Paris is a major European city and a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. It is well-known for its iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, the Notre-Dame Cathedral, and many more. Established in the 3rd century BC, Paris has a rich history that extends over 2,000 years and has been an influential city throughout that time. Today, Paris is a leading tourist destination, attracting millions of visitors from around the world each year.
